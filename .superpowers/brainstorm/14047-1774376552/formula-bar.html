Key Details

Formula bar — shows cell address (B2), token name, and the full editable formula in a CodeMirror instance with autocomplete. Always visible, never truncated.
Cell sizing — fixed column widths, white-space: nowrap + text-overflow: ellipsis. No wrapping ever. Columns resizable by dragging header borders.
Color swatches — compact 14px squares inline with hex value. In resolved column only. Never wrap to a new line.
Drag-fill handle — 7px blue square at bottom-right corner of selected cell/range. Drag down/right to fill series.
